“Chrissy, always remember that truly smart people know that they always have something new to learn.”

This quote comes from my dad.
For the past five years, I have been a High School Geometry Teacher. I began each school year by writing a program with my students on our TI calculators. My goal was to show the students how to break down a task with profound problem-solving skills. Once the students saw it working, they were so impressed! Before I knew it, I became the touchpoint as students went on to write their own programs. Troubleshooting with them turned out to be the most energetic part of my day. I was trying to inspire them, but I found them inspiring me! I thought to myself, I really love teaching, but is programming something I love even more? So, I decided to make a career change! I am eager, enthusiastic, and excited for this journey!
